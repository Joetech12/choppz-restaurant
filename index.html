Welcome To Choppz Restaurant

Since our modest beginnings in 2012 with a little space in Lekki Phase 1, Choppz Restaurant‘s development has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve solid, Local and Foreign-roused takeout food. In contrast to other eateries, Choppz Restaurant was made with the explicit expectation to appear as something else.

Choppz Restaurant has developed to incorporate four superb takeout areas in Lagos with additional to come sooner rather than later. Our group takes pride in the way that we can furnish our new and faithful clients with extraordinary tasting Local and Foreign-roused nourishment that is not normal for that at some other eatery you visit.
